What Ping Does

Ping asks one simple question each day.

Sometimes it is fun. Sometimes it is reflective. Sometimes it is surprisingly useful. The point is not to measure people to death. The point is to create a small daily habit that helps people know and be known.

After answering, you can see:

  • how the group answered today
  • who matched your answer
  • how your own responses are trending over time
  • what Ping’s AI notices about the team as a whole

No long surveys. No giant engagement platform. No waiting six months for a report nobody reads.

Just one question, asked and answered.

Why It Exists

Most employee surveys feel like they belong to leadership.

Ping is different. Ping is built to feel like it belongs to the team.

The questions are lightweight enough to answer every day, but meaningful enough to start real conversations. Over time, those small answers become a clearer picture of how the team is doing, what people value, and where the workplace might need attention.

Ping is serious insight wrapped in a much more human experience.

How It Works

1

Answer the daily Ping

Open the app, answer one question, and move on with your day.

2

See the results instantly

After you answer, you can see how others responded. It is quick, social, and sometimes unexpectedly funny.

3

Notice your matches

Ping can show when you and someone else have answered the same way over a streak of days. It is a small way to find common ground with people you may not know well yet.

4

Learn from the patterns

Ping uses AI to summarize broad team-level themes and trends without calling out individuals. The goal is to help the team understand itself better, not put anyone on trial.

Built for Teams That Don’t Need Another Survey

Ping is for teams that want more connection, more conversation, and more honest signals about how people are doing.

It is especially useful for:

  • small and mid-sized companies
  • distributed or hybrid teams
  • leaders who want better insight without survey fatigue
  • teams that want culture-building to feel less forced

Ping is not trying to replace every HR tool. It is trying to become the question people actually talk about.

Privacy and Safety

Ping is designed to make team patterns visible without turning individual answers into management ammunition.

Team insights are written at the group level. The AI is instructed not to single out individuals, diagnose people, or identify someone as the source of a problem.

The match feature is designed to create connection, not exposure. It shows shared answers and common ground, especially the kind that can help people feel less alone.

Beta Testing
